Why use Graphite?

Unlike other web based document editors, Graphite is designed for speed. With a minimalistic user interface and fast loading times, Graphite is the ideal editor for taking notes, writing reports or essays, and composing poetry.


Graphite gives you the freedom of paper, with the ease of use and power of a computer, it allows you to create documents and notes with ease, and felxibility.


Best of all, Graphite is free for everyone, for ever. We will never show you ads, and we will never share your information with anyone.
