About

Why did we create Graphite?

Graphite is a passion project made by Ronan.F with help from Sasha.S. We made Graphite because we saw a problem, other web-based text editors took ages to load. In response we decided to make Graphite, our goals were to make a simple, secure, and fast web-based text editor for FREE. When we created Graphite we decided that we would not display ads or sell your data.
